Naval Ravikant's Family







Naval Ravikant belongs to a Hindu family. Naval Ravikant’s father worked as a pharmacist in India who later worked at a hardware store in New York. Naval Ravikant got married to Krystle Cho, a visual designer, in 2013 and they have a son named Neo.
